# LLM-Based CX Evaluation Suite

## Overview
This repository provides a **CX-native evaluation framework** for LLM-powered conversational AI systems, designed for enterprise platforms.

Traditional NLP metrics do not reflect customer experience. This suite evaluates **entire conversations**, not individual responses.

---

## What This Evaluates
- Resolution correctness
- Hallucination risk
- Intent consistency across turns
- Confidence calibration
- Escalation appropriateness
- Customer effort & friction

---

## Evaluation Pipeline
Conversation Logs  
→ Turn-level Analysis  
→ Conversation-level Aggregation  
→ CX Quality Metrics  
→ Dashboards & Reports

---

## Core Metrics
- Resolution Accuracy Score
- Hallucination Risk Index
- Intent Consistency Score
- Confidence Stability Score
- Escalation Timing Quality

---

## Why This Matters
Enterprise customers don’t care if a reply “sounds good”.
They care if the **problem was resolved safely**.

---

## Target Audience
- CX analytics teams
- AI evaluation engineers
